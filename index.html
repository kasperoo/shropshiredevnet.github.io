About Shropshire Dev Net

Free tickets!

Shropshire dev net is a free to attend user group which is made possible by our great sponsors ProtoLabs and the eInnovation centre, as well as our community organisers.

Expert Speakers

We have amazing speakers from accross the country who give their time for free to speak on the latest web development technologies and practices.

Pizza!

Free pizza is provided by our sponsors for all events, because what would a developers user group be without pizza!?
